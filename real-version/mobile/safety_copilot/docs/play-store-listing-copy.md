## Full Description
Safety Copilot helps families and trusted contacts stay connected during daily travel.

Use it every day for proactive safety visibility, and instantly during emergencies.

What you can do:
- Create trusted circles (family, friends, roommates)
- Start a trip with destination and ETA context
- Share live trip status with trusted members
- Trigger SOS or Silent SOS when needed
- Auto-detect arrival and notify your trusted circle
- View safety alerts (offline risk, low battery, route deviation, delay)
- Acknowledge alerts so everyone knows they were seen

Why Safety Copilot:
- Built for real daily life, not just emergencies
- Designed for quick actions under stress
- Clean, readable interface with fast interactions
- Internal-testing-first reliability approach

Safety Copilot is intended to support personal safety awareness and coordination. It is not a replacement for emergency services.
