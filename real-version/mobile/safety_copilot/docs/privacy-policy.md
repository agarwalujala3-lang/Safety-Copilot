# Privacy Policy - Safety Copilot

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Safety Copilot ("we", "our", "us") provides trusted-circle travel safety features, including trip status sharing, alerts, and SOS workflows.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information needed to provide core app functionality.

### 1.1 Account Information
- Name
- Phone number
- Password (stored as a secure hash, not plain text)

### 1.2 Safety and Trip Data
- Precise location during trip monitoring and safety heartbeats
- Destination details and trip status (active, arrived, ended)
- Safety events and alerts (for example SOS, silent SOS, low battery, route risk)
- Optional notes provided during SOS events

### 1.3 Device and Reliability Data
- App-generated device identifier
- Push token (when push messaging is enabled)
- App version, platform, battery level, charging state
- Heartbeat timestamps used to detect offline risk

## 2. How We Use Data
We use collected data to:
- Authenticate users and protect account access
- Connect users with trusted circles
- Provide live trip visibility and arrival notifications
- Trigger and display safety alerts and SOS workflows
- Improve reliability and operational safety monitoring

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data.

Safety data is shared only within your trusted circle as required by core app functionality.
We may use infrastructure providers to host and process data on our behalf.

## 4. Data Retention
We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, safety history, legal requirements, and fraud/security prevention.

## 5. Security
We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including encrypted transport (HTTPS) and controlled backend access.

## 6. Your Choices
- You can stop location sharing by ending an active trip.
- You can disable app notifications in device settings.
- You may request account/data deletion by contacting us.

## 7. Children
Safety Copilot is not directed to children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by revising the effective date.

## 9. Contact Us
For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact:
- Email: privacy@safetycopilot.app
