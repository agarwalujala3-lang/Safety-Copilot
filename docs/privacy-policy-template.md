# Safety Copilot Privacy Policy (Template)

Last updated: 2026-04-10

## Data we collect
1. Account data: name, phone number.
2. Device data: app/device identifiers, FCM token.
3. Safety data: trip events, live location during active sharing, SOS events.
4. Diagnostics: crash and performance logs.

## Why we collect data
1. Provide trusted-circle safety monitoring.
2. Trigger safety alerts (arrival, SOS, risk alerts).
3. Improve reliability and prevent abuse.

## Data sharing
1. Shared only with trusted circle members you approve.
2. Shared with cloud providers strictly for hosting/notification delivery.
3. Not sold to third parties.

## Location permissions
1. Foreground location: required for active trip tracking.
2. Background location: required for continuous safety monitoring during active trip.

## Retention and deletion
1. Users can request account and trip data deletion.
2. Logs retained only for operational and safety requirements.
